Learn More About Breast Cancer

What is Breast Cancer?

It is a disease where malignant cells form in the breast tissue. Early detection and treatment are crucial for better outcomes.

Risk Factors

Age, family history, BRCA1/2 gene mutations, lifestyle choices like alcohol use and obesity can increase risk.

Symptoms

Common symptoms include lumps, nipple discharge, and skin changes. Regular self-exams and checkups help in early detection.

Prevention

Healthy diet, regular exercise, limiting alcohol, and routine screenings are key preventive measures.
